Road Bikes

Designed for speed on paved roads, these bikes have lightweight frames, narrow tires, and dropped handlebars. They are popular among commuters and enthusiasts who prioritize speed and efficiency.

Hybrid Bikes

Combining features of road and mountain bikes, hybrids offer versatility for both paved and unpaved surfaces. They have medium-width tires, comfortable seating positions, and moderate suspension, making them suitable for city commuting and light off-road use.

Electric Bikes

Equipped with an electric motor to assist with pedaling, e-bikes are gaining popularity in urban areas for reducing the effort needed to navigate long distances or hilly terrains. They are seen as an eco-friendly alternative to motorbikes and cars.

Folding Bikes

Designed for easy storage and portability, folding bikes are compact and can be folded into a smaller size. They are ideal for city dwellers who need to combine cycling with public transportation or have limited storage space.

Fixed Gear/Single Speed Bikes

Simple bikes with a single gear, known for their minimalistic design and ease of maintenance. They are popular among urban cyclists who prefer a straightforward and reliable bike.
